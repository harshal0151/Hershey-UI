Zero Sugar

Trying to cut back on sugar and still enjoy your favorite candy and snacks? We offer a variety of Zero Sugar products across our chocolate candy, sweets and gum. They are sweetened with sugar alcohols and provide about 20% fewer calories per serving than our classic versions but are not necessarily low-calorie foods. Sugar alcohols (or polyols), contain some calories and increase blood sugar levels to varying degrees. Like with other food ingredients, such as gluten and lactose, some of us may be more sensitive to sugar alcohols and experience an upset stomach. We recommend enjoying Zero Sugar candy in moderation and only consuming one serving at a time to avoid potential discomfort.
